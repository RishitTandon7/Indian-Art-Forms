Urban Folk Renaissance

Born near the Kalighat Temple during Kali Puja, these paintings blend divine devotion with sharp social satire. Bold lines and flat colors capture gods, goddesses, and the evolving life of Kolkata.

This revolutionary art form marked the transition from traditional folk painting to modern urban expression, reflecting the changing social landscape of colonial Bengal.
