Sacred Clay Guardians

Forged in clay for the Vishwakarma Puja fairs of West Bengal, these horses stand tall with proud necks and geometric patterns. They are symbols of prosperity, guardians of tradition, and enduring testaments to rural craftsmanship.

Each piece embodies centuries of devotion, where skilled artisans transform humble clay into divine protectors that grace Bengali homes and temples during sacred celebrations.
