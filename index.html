Pattachitra Painting ‚Äì Odisha

Pattachitra emerges as Odisha's most revered scroll painting tradition, born from devotion to Lord Jagannath during the magnificent Rath Yatra festival. These vibrant masterpieces weave together tales from Hindu epics and local folklore using natural pigments and intricate detailing. Artists preserve this sacred legacy through generations, creating visual prayers that connect earthly celebrations with divine narratives.

Kalighat Painting ‚Äì Kolkata

Born in the sacred precincts of Kolkata's Kalighat temple, this revolutionary art form brilliantly merged ancient folk traditions with emerging urban sensibilities during Kali Puja celebrations. This transformative style marked India's artistic evolution while maintaining deep spiritual roots in temple worship and festival traditions.
